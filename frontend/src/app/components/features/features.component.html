WebSite Features
Current Features
  • Customer or Tailor Registration with Email and Password.
  • Cutomers and Tailor Both can edit their custom details.
  • Customers can search the tailors and visit their pages and explore the categories and book appointment for a selected category.
  • Customer can trach their their appointment status.
  • Tailor can add, edit and delete the categories from their home page.
  • Tailor can view all the recieved appointments and and manage them whether to accept, decline or change the date for the requested appointment.
  • If an appointment is accepted then tailor can record the measurements and payments manually from the appoinment details page.
Upcoming Features
  • Email verification before the registration to register only genuine users.
  • Email notifications services to update the appointment status time to time to the customers.
  • Payment Gateway to Accept the online payments.
